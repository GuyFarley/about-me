About Me

Biography

Hello, my name is Guy. I was born and raised in Northern California, but now I call Oregon home. I like to keep my life pretty simple, and focus all my energy on just a few things that I find rewarding. These include a few strong friendships, time outdoors, and work that is fulfilling.

Education

Long before Code Fellows, I graduated from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo with a bachelor's degree in Graphic Communication. Go Mustangs.

Job Experience

My career started in print production, and from there I made my way into project management. I lived in Nashville, TN for a while, where I managed packaging design projects for Dollar General's private brands. When I came back out west, I jumped back into print production for a bit, then spent the past five years managing print procurement for Columbia Sportswear and Unilever. Most recently, I managed a team of project managers in support of Unilever's retail display production.

Goals

My primary goal is to find a career path that is rewarding to me, offers constant learning opportunities, and leaves me energized. I would also like to buy a small house that is conveniently located to some rivers and trails, and has a back yard for my dog to run around in.
